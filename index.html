The IIT Shortcut — Do This Before Any Cold Message
Search LinkedIn for IIT alumni at every target company. If you find one, DM them first. This converts to a warm intro to the decision maker 60–70% of the time and cuts your sales cycle from 3 weeks to 1 week.

Discovery Call Questions

Open with: "Tell me — where does your ops team waste the most time on something that shouldn't need a human?"

#QuestionWhat you're probing for
1What are the 3 most time-consuming manual tasks your team does weekly?The top 1–2 workflows to target
2Are there reports that require multiple people to compile? How long?How painful is it — time × people × frequency
3What do you track in Excel that has grown "out of control"?The specific Excel asset they're ashamed of
4If I could give your team 10 hrs back per week — what would you automate first?Their highest-priority = your Pilot scope
5What happens when the person who "knows the spreadsheet" is on leave?How fragile is the current system
6Have you tried solving this before? What happened?Why past attempts failed = your differentiation

Portfolio Before First Client

You have zero client work. These 3 assets bridge the credibility gap.

BCG Case Study Do first
Anonymize your GPT-powered Excel automation from BCG. "Reduced report generation from 8 hrs to 45 mins for a ₹1,300 Cr battery OEM." Real, credible, directly relevant. This is your strongest asset.
Demo Project
Pick one industry. Build a fake company's before/after — show the Excel chaos, then the clean dashboard. 15 mins of build = a 5-minute demo that converts cold leads to warm conversations.
Friend's Startup Cases
2–3 anonymized data points from your co-founder's startup transforms your pitch from "two guys" to "proven team." Even screenshots with company names blurred work.
